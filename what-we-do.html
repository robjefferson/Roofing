Flat roofing

We offer a variety of material to cover ‘flat’ roof areas including…
Multi layer built up felt roof coverings. Traditional ‘Pour-and-roll’ as well as Anderson high performance ‘Torch-application’ systems.
Lead coverings and flashings to conform to the lead development agency guidelines and standards.

Lead alternative materials are also available for situations where theft of the material from exposed positions can lead to undesirable water ingress and costly, repeated, insurance claims.

GRP alternatives are available for situations where conformation with Lead Development Association (LDA) regulations cannot be achieved without costly structural alterations.

With the option to incorporate insulation material, roof void ventilation roof windows and other associated works within the system our reliable and efficient service to clients with ‘flat’ roof coverings is exceptional.

Domestic & Private

Taking the worry of building control and other regulations away from you, we can offer a service to accommodate all types of grant work and replacement roof coverings.

Many people enter into ‘self-build’ projects where they co-ordinate all the various trades throughout the building project, from clearing a site, excavating for foundations, arranging for bricklayers, joiners, plumbers etc and the supply of materials; all to arrive on time and to be completed without causing delays to the many follow-on trades.

To employ us to install the roof coverings, can take some of the stress off your shoulders. With advance site visits, liaison with other trades and our usual attention to the smaller details.

No job too small

Gale-force winds, storms and severe weather conditions are never welcome. The damage caused by these conditions can vary tremendously, from the odd tile or ridge blowing off the roof to chimney stacks toppling over and falling through the roof to complete areas of both tiled and flat roofs being blown to the ground.

Restoring the integrity of the roof coverings to minimise the risk of further damage is of prime importance to us, as well as you and your insurer.

Ashbridge Roofing Solutions can offer the facility to speak directly with your insurance companie to help determine the extent of any loss and to return your roof to a weather-tight condition at the earliest opportunity.

We also undertake routine repair and maintenance works, clearing leaves and debris from gutters.
