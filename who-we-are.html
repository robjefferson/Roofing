Who we are

We're a long-established, local business with a strong reputation and highly skilled workforce.

The full story

Ashbridge Roofing Solutions has roots which date back to the 1980s. Originally established in Grimsby, the business started as a roofing merchant, selling materials to the general public for DIY projects as well as to established trade business.

During the 1990s the merchanting aspect of the business declined but the contracting side maintained its reputation as a trustworthy, reliable service offering sound technical advice and roofing craftsmanship. The company later joined forces with Bridge Roofing in Scunthorpe, already an established force for solid reliability, quality and technical experience in the area.

Today, we are part of ongo, a local organisation whose main aim is create and sustain truly vibrant communities. In essence, nothing has really changed. We are still Ashbridge Roofing Solutions, with the same staff. The only difference now is that our profits will help ongo deliver its promise that everything they do is put back into local communities.

We will continue to work from our current base on Northampton Road, in Scunthorpe, for the foreseeable future, and also have a satellite operation in Grimsby.
